Classic Roast Chicken

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Whole mutton 1 (about 4 pounds)
Olive Oil 2 tablespoons
Salt 1 teaspoon
Black Pepper 1/2 teaspoon
Garlic 4 cloves, minced
Lemon 1, halved
Fresh Herbs (thyme, rosemary, parsley) 1 bunch

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425째F (220째C).
  2. Rinse the chicken inside and out and pat dry with paper towels.
  3. Rub the chicken with olive oil, then season with salt and pepper.
  4. Stuff the cavity with garlic, lemon halves, and fresh herbs.
  5. Place the chicken on a roasting pan and roast for about 1 hour and 20 minutes, or until the juices run clear when the thigh is pierced with a knife.
  6. Let the chicken rest for 10-15 minutes before carving.
